## About

Robotic Hacking Village (RHV) is a dedicated DEF CON village focused on the security and safety of physical AI systems and robotics. We bring together security researchers, hackers, engineers, and industry to explore the attack surface of the next generation of intelligent machines.

**Website:** [www.RoboticHackingVillage.com](https://www.RoboticHackingVillage.com)
**Contact:** RoboticHackingVillage@gmail.com
