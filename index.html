What Is Robotic Hacking Village?

A dedicated DEF CON village focused on the security and safety of physical AI systems and robotics — machines that move, sense, decide, and act in the real world.


We bring together security researchers, hackers, engineers, and industry to explore the attack surface of the next generation of intelligent machines. Because a compromise is not just a data breach — it can cause physical harm.

  • Robots are no longer science fiction — they are in factories, warehouses, hospitals, and homes
  • Physical AI systems introduce an entirely new and underexplored attack surface
  • Security research must keep pace with the speed of deployment
  • Safety and security must be engineered in — not bolted on
  • The hacker community is best positioned to find what defenders miss
